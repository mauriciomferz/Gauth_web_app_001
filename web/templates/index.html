What is GAuth?

GAuth is a next-generation authorization framework designed specifically for AI systems that need to act on behalf of humans with explicit delegation and full auditability.

Explicit Delegation

Clear, verifiable power-of-attorney flows with transparent authorization chains

Full Auditability

Complete transparency in authorization decisions with structured logging

AI-Native Design

Built specifically for modern AI systems and automated workflows

RFC Compliance

Adheres to GiFo-RFC-0111 and GiFo-RFC-0115 standards
